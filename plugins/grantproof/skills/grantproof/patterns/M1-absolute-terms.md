# M1 — Absolute terms

**Trigger words** (canonical list in `../triggers.json`): *always, every, all, completely, guarantee, the best, the most, revolutionary*

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

Nothing in the claim can be tested. The reviewer either dismisses it as marketing or looks for a single counter-example to attack the whole claim.

## Rewrite structure

`[Your product / system] [achieved / demonstrated / produced] [your measured outcome] on [named metric] in [defined sample], compared to [named comparator condition].`
