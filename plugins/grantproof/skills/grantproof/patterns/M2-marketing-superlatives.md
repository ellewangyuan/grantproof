# M2 — Marketing superlatives

**Trigger words** (canonical list in `../triggers.json`): *unparalleled, breakthrough, game-changing*

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

Marketing vocabulary is opaque to mixed-domain reviewers. The panelist scoring the proposal may not be in your field — these words anchor nothing they can picture or score.

## Rewrite structure

`[Your specific technical capability — name the actual feature, not the marketing label] [verb] [measurable performance characteristic] under [defined conditions], evaluated against [named comparator or baseline].`
