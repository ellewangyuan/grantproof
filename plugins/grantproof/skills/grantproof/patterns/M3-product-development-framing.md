# M3 — Product-development framing

**Trigger phrases** (canonical list in `../triggers.json`): *spin out of, version 2 of, fork of, build out the X feature*

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

Reads as product development, not R&D. Government R&D grants fund knowledge generation and technical risk-taking, not feature iteration on a shipped product.

## Rewrite structure

`We propose to investigate whether [phenomenon] correlates with [outcome] under [defined conditions].`
