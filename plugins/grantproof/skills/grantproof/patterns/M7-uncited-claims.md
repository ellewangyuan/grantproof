# M7 — Uncited factual claims

**Trigger pattern**: Any specific factual claim presented without a source — dollar figures, percentages, durations, trend statements, comparative market claims, "industry data shows…" without naming the industry source.

This is a semantic pattern, not a literal word match.

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

Reviewers cannot evaluate ungrounded claims. Even non-specialist panelists trust references over assertions, and an unsourced figure invites either silent skepticism or an active fact-check.

## Rewrite structure

`[Your claim] ([citation — name the peer-reviewed paper, government report, or industry data source]).`

## Note on application

Do not invent citations. If the founder doesn't have a source, prompt them to replace the claim with a defensible figure they do have, or remove the claim.
