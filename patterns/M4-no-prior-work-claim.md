# M4 — "Nobody has done this before"

**Trigger phrase**: *"Nobody has done this before"* and close variants — the no-anchor / "no prior work" class of claim.

See `../triggers.json` for the canonical variant list.

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

Counter-intuitively backfires. Saying "nobody has done this" gives reviewers no anchor and invites them to find prior work themselves to attack the claim. Reviewers reward founders who can place their work on a clear map of past credible efforts.

## Rewrite structure

`Prior work in [adjacent domain] has [established result — with citation]; our R&D addresses the open question of [the specific gap your work fills].`
