# M5 — Vague impact language

**Trigger pattern**: *"will help"* + a vague noun — and the broader class of "if we do this, something good will happen" statements without measurable consequence.

This is a semantic pattern, not a literal word match. `../triggers.json` lists representative phrases; detection should also catch close variants of the same framing.

## Why this lands wrong with reviewers

The framing rule for impact is *"if we don't do this, something extremely terrible will happen"* — never *"if we do this, something good will happen."* Vague benefit language cannot be measured, and reviewers cannot reward an impact claim they can't verify.

## Rewrite structure

`If [your specific technical challenge] is not resolved, [the consequence — with citable scale where one exists].`
